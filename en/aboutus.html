Who am i ?

My name's Ethan, and I'm currently doing a multimedia and internet diploma at the IUT in Montbéliard. As part of this course, we've been asked to choose a concept or an object and then create a website around it. on the theme of the prehistory of the future. For this S1 project, I chose to focus on an area that I've long been passionate about I've long been passionate about, cinema.

The concept

The idea behind this project is to create a site that would take us back to a hundred years ago. At that time, with the impact the arts will have been superseded. Everyone will be able to imitate the artist of their choice with a simple prompt. At first consumers will find this brilliant and will no longer go to cinemas, art galleries or the like, preferring instead to their own films, paintings, illustrations, etc., but before long the work of AIs will be perceived as soulless, bland and meaningless, and so will the work of artists, bland and devoid of meaning, and so everyone will gradually abandon the arts (and, by the same token, cinema). It is in this context that this site would imagine our influenced vision of the 7th art, as a fact where the latest series bringing together not enthusiasts of a lost art.
